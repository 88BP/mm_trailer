LOVE THEM, HATE THEM, THEY'RE HERE.

QNA Group and 88 Brand Partners, two of Chicago’s leading research and communications firms, teamed up to uncover exactly how Millennials interact with service-related brands. Through our research, we found who they are, how they buy, how they think, how they interact, what they value and what it all means to you. This study aims to give service brands a better idea of how to refine their marketing approach to more successfully reach and engage with this misunderstood group, which just so happens to be the most valuable segment in today’s marketplace.
